Best BlackJack strategy:
Each table corresponds to a specific type of hand: hard hands, soft hands, and pairs. The columns on the top of the tables represent the ​dealer's upcard, which influences the ​player's strategy. The tables simplify the strategy by grouping ​Jack, ​Queen, and ​King cards with the ​Ten card, as they all have a value of 10. To use the tables, a player needs to determine whether they have a pair, soft hand, or hard hand. Then, they can find the appropriate table and locate the cell that corresponds to their hand holding and the dealer's upcard. The cell will indicate the recommended strategy: "H" for hit, "S" for stand, "D" for double-down, and "P" for split (only in the pairs table).
